Indian GroceryIndustries Limited has prevailed as the radical catalyst to serve and evolve the Indian retailers' community. The rate of increase of Unorganised retailers is soaring high and has reached a stressful amount of 1.1 crores. They withstand heavy baggage of not being acknowledged in the market and suffer tremendously to manage revenue and finance. Still, under IGIL, any unorganized retailer can enroll his business to gain links and have a full area of exposure to the market. IGIL works by the vision and cooperation of all retailers, suppliers, manufacturers, consumers, and investors. There is no exaggeration in the fact that IGIL is a one-way solution of all the difficulties that revolve around retailing.

The godown mart of the IGIL community is where Management meets Implementation. This brand name can be accessed by high-end consumers so that they can be provided with every single product or service they require.

Godown Mart is also a ray of hope for unorganised retailers. The ones who are unrecognized and are fighting for survival in the market continuously. These unorganised retailers can join hands with the godown mart within which they will be labelled as Organised and from which they will incur all the facilities that we offer.

From the advertisement of their stores to centralized management, from controlling the product supply chain to providing transportation facilities, we also handle the client input along with finance and revenue management. We are here to give them all it takes to transform them into a self-reliant and organised store.
